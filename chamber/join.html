Benefits of Becoming a Member

Come and be part of the Largest Community of People who are making a difference in the city of Ndola!

Each membership level has its own benefits as explained below!

Benefits of an NP Member

By becoming a member,you also get free access to executive trainings where you will learn more about good business techniques and ideas on how to improve your business efficiency.
Fill in the form below to become to Join!

Benefits of a Bronze Member

In addition to the NP Membership,When you join the Ndola Chamber of Commerce as a Bronze member,you become part of the largest community of local business of ndola who are always working work to improve their business through improved revenues and good business relationships.

Benefits of an a Silver Member

In addition to the Bronze Membership,When you join the Ndola Chamber of Commerce as a Silver member,you will have access to executive training and monthly business meetings for the chamber.

Benefits of a Gold Member

In addition to the Silver Membership,When you join the Ndola Chamber of Commerce as a Gold member,you become legible to advertising (on our home page spotlights), and event discounts
