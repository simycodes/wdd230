Scenarios when a user can visit the website

  • A local new business owner looking for ways to grow his business and develop new and lasting business relationships that can help increase his customer numbers and revenue.
  • A Ndola city resident (and any other user) wanting to learn more about a city chamber of commerce
  • A student wanting to find out more about companies that are doing well in delivering their services or products and which look attractive to work for.
  • A tourist or a new person in town from another country or province looking to find a specific product or service such as photography or tourist sites that can be seen by visiting local established businesses.
  • An internal company looking for similar businesses that exist locally and tries to find out about common business polices and operations as it plans to get established as also.
