Site Purpose

The purpose of the The Ndola City Chamber of Commerce is to unite all the local small business of the city especially new start ups so they can work together in a friendly environment that is run by efficient business policies and interests created and agreed upon by all local business.The Chamber of commerce will help businesses to support each other and grow through different work-shops and the creation of lasting business relationships among business owners and employees.
